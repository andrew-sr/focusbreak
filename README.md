# FocusBreak

A free 20-20-20 eye rest timer for people who spend too long staring at screens.

👉 **[focusbreak.app](https://focusbreak.app)**

---

## What is the 20-20-20 rule?

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. It's a simple habit recommended by optometrists worldwide to reduce digital eye strain, dry eyes, and screen fatigue.

## What FocusBreak does

FocusBreak runs quietly in your browser and reminds you to take eye breaks at the right intervals. No download, no account, no subscription. Just open it and let it run while you work.

- Customisable work and break intervals
- Gentle audio and visual reminders
- Tracks your sessions and breaks throughout the day
- Works on any device with a browser
- Completely free, no ads

## Who it's for

Remote workers, developers, gamers, students — anyone logging long hours in front of a screen.
